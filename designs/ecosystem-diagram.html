A note on the Financial Planning pillar

Unlike AI-assisted features elsewhere in the platform, the financial planning pillar is built around human expertise. Families are connected directly to verified financial advisors, CFPs, and estate planners from the PCG Trusted Directory — real professionals who specialize in elder care costs, asset protection, Medicaid planning, and long-term care financing.

PCG provides the tools to organize and track financial information. The guidance comes from people.
