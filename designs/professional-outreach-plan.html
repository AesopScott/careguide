Outreach phases & timeline

Month 1–2

Foundation — build the list

Identify and vet prospective professionals before any outreach begins. Quality over quantity at this stage sets the tone for the entire directory.

  • Compile lists of CFPs, elder law attorneys, and RIAs from NAPFA, NAELA, and state bar directories
  • Filter for elder care specialization, geography, and client reviews
  • Build a CRM with contact details, specialty, and region
  • Draft outreach email templates (see below) and LinkedIn messaging scripts
  • Create a professional-facing landing page explaining the program
Month 3–4

First wave — warm outreach

Lead with a free listing offer to build the directory. The goal is density — families won't use a directory with three names in it.

  • Send personalized emails to the top 100 prospects — no mass blasts
  • Connect with targets on LinkedIn before emailing when possible
  • Offer founding member free listings in exchange for a testimonial and social share
  • Follow up at 7 and 14 days with a different angle (not just "checking in")
  • Target: 30 confirmed free listings
Month 5–6

Second wave — association outreach

Stop reaching professionals one at a time and start reaching the groups they belong to. One partnership with a professional association can yield dozens of listings.

  • Pitch NAPFA, NAELA, and AARP to distribute PCG as a recommended tool
  • Offer to speak at chapter meetings or contribute to member newsletters
  • Target eldercare-focused CPE webinars for CFP and CPA audiences
  • Partner with hospital social work departments for resource distribution
  • Target: 50 total listings, first premium upgrades
Month 7+

Sustained growth — referral flywheel

At this point the directory should begin growing itself. Families refer professionals. Professionals refer colleagues. The directory becomes self-sustaining.

  • Launch a "Refer a colleague" program for listed professionals
  • Begin paid promotion of the directory to families via Google and Facebook ads
  • Introduce tiered pricing for enhanced listings
  • Quarterly outreach to new graduates from CFP and eldercare certification programs
