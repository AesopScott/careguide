How it works for you

PCG is built for every person in the care circle. Select your role to see your experience.

You're already doing everything.
We make it possible to keep track.

You didn't sign up to be a care coordinator, insurance navigator, and medication manager all at once. You're a Care Advocate — and PCG gives you one place to see everything, with a professional team supporting you through it.

Join the waitlist
1
Get invited or sign up directly
Your care manager sends you an invite link, or you can join PCG directly. Either way, your account is tied to your care recipient's plan from day one.
2
See the full picture in one place
Diagnoses, medications, care goals, urgent flags — all organized and current. No more calling three different offices to find out what's happening.
3
Access the crisis card instantly
In an emergency, every critical detail — allergies, DNR status, emergency contacts, insurance — is one tap away. No searching, no panic, no delay.
4
Stay connected to the care team
Message your care coordinator directly. Get updates when the care plan changes. Know what's happening without having to chase anyone down.
5
Feel less alone in it
Advocacy is isolating. PCG wraps your whole team — professionals and family — around a single shared plan so you're never the only one who knows everything.

Your expertise deserves tools
that actually keep up.

You manage complex cases across dozens of families. PCG gives you an AI-assisted intake, a structured care plan, and a family portal — so your expertise reaches every Care Advocate and care recipient who needs it.

1
Sign up and set your profile

2

5
Manage your full caseload
Your client roster shows every family, care level, and urgent flag at a glance. Prioritize and update without the administrative overhead.

Legal planning that stays
connected to the care plan.

Estate planning doesn't end when the documents are signed. PCG keeps you connected to the care team so the legal plan and care plan stay in sync — and Care Advocates know their care recipient's affairs are in order.

Create a practitioner account
1
Sign up with your legal specialty
Create your account as an elder law attorney. Your client view is tailored to the documents and milestones that matter most in your practice.
2
Track legal documents for each care recipient
Powers of attorney, advance directives, DNR status, wills, and trusts — tied to the care record so nothing gets lost when it matters most.
3
Keep Care Advocates informed
Invite the family to view the legal document summary in their portal. They know what exists and where to find it — without a call every time.
4
Coordinate with the care team
When the care plan changes — a new diagnosis, a transition to memory care — you're in the loop. Legal decisions and care decisions stay aligned.
5
Document legal milestones
Track when documents are executed, updated, or need review. Families always know the status, and you have a clear record for your practice.

More time for the work that
only you can do.

The documentation burden in social work is relentless. PCG handles the structure so you can focus on the relationship — AI-assisted intake, care plan drafts, and a portal that keeps Care Advocates aligned with the plan.

Create a practitioner account
1
Sign up and build your client roster
Create your practitioner account and start adding care recipients. Each one gets their own care record, family portal, and care team — fully separate and secure.
2
Run an AI-assisted intake
Walk through a structured five-part intake — client context, current situation, medical history, care needs, and goals. PCG generates a care plan draft you refine with your clinical judgment.
3
Connect families to the right resources
Document community resources, referrals, and interventions in the care plan. Care Advocates can see what's been arranged and what's coming next.
4
Track care transitions
When a care recipient moves from home to assisted living, or hospital to rehab, update their care level and plan in one place. Everyone on the team sees it immediately.
5
Document outcomes over time
Session notes, goal progress, and plan revisions are all logged and timestamped. Your work is visible, documented, and defensible — without the paperwork pile.
